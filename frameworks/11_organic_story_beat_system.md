# Organic Story Beat System

## Metadata
- Type: Framework
- Domain: Story Structure / Beat Flow / Controlled Variation
- Status: active
- Priority: high

---

## Purpose
Build flexible story movement without locking every story into the same template.

---

## Beat Types
Use as functions, not fixed steps.

- **Opening disturbance:** quiet wrongness appears early
- **Foundation:** routine, setting, and narrator credibility
- **Intrigue amplifier:** a detail raises more questions than it answers
- **False stability:** narrator believes the problem is manageable
- **Escalation:** pressure increases in scope, proximity, or consequence
- **Pattern break:** the established logic shifts unexpectedly
- **Hidden setup:** a detail matters later without announcing itself
- **Convergence:** separate details begin to connect
- **Identity/reality threat:** self, memory, space, or certainty breaks
- **Resolution/reframe:** ending answers less than it implies

---

## Rules
- Do not expose beat labels in final story.
- Do not use the same beat order every run.
- Use pattern breaks sparingly and with setup.
- Major twists must be subtly prepared earlier.
- Ending should feel inevitable in hindsight, not random.

---

## Success Standard
The story should feel organic to the listener while remaining structurally controlled underneath.
