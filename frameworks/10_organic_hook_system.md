# Organic Hook System

## Metadata
- Type: Framework
- Domain: Opening / Retention / Horror Tone
- Status: active
- Priority: high

---

## Purpose
Create early curiosity without clickbait tone. Horror hooks should feel like natural story openings.

---

## Hook Types

- **Contradiction:** a normal fact contains quiet wrongness
- **Delayed awareness:** narrator did not understand significance at first
- **Confession:** reluctant admission, not performance
- **Normalcy disruption:** routine disturbed by one small detail
- **Retrospective:** narrator now knows an ordinary moment mattered

---

## Rules
- Hook within the first 1–3 lines.
- Raise a question; do not answer it.
- Preserve narrator voice and realism.
- Avoid exaggerated claims, forced urgency, or generic “you won’t believe” language.
- Vary hook type across stories.

---

## Success Standard
The opening should feel like something a real person would say while still making the viewer want the next line.
