# STRUCTURED Output Schema

## Story [##] — [Functional Title]

### Entities
List only explicit entities from the story.

### Sequence of Events
Ordered events only. Preserve sequence. Do not compress major events.

### Key Actions
Direct actions taken by entities. Must be grounded in text.

### Signals / Red Flags
Observable behaviors only. No interpretation or psychology labels.

### Outcome
Explicit outcome from the story. No lesson or inferred meaning.
