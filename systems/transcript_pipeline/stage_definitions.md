# Transcript Pipeline — Stage Definitions

## RAW
Structure source transcripts into metadata and story sections. No rewriting, summary, interpretation, or content removal.

## CLEANED
Light cleanup only. Preserve wording, voice, tone, and story segmentation.

## STRUCTURED
Extraction only. Extract entities, sequence, actions, observable signals, and outcomes from explicit text.

## DISTILLED
Grounded abstraction only. Extract literal, observable patterns and mechanics from STRUCTURED output.

## INDEXED
Inactive in v1_validated. Do not execute or write back yet.
