# Test Seeds — Baseline Set

## Purpose
These seeds are used for consistent system validation and regression testing.

---

## Seed 01 — Spatial Intrusion
A woman notices objects in her apartment slightly shifting position each morning, but only when she sleeps.

---

## Seed 02 — Impossible Communication
A man starts receiving replies to text messages he never sent.

---

## Seed 03 — Reflection Discrepancy
A child insists that their reflection is “not copying correctly” and begins avoiding mirrors.

---

## Seed 04 — Pattern Disruption
Every night at exactly 3:12 AM, footsteps are heard in the hallway—until one night, they start earlier.

---

## Seed 05 — External Awareness
Someone overhears strangers discussing their private thoughts in public places.

---

## Usage Rules
- Do not modify seeds during testing
- Use consistently before and after system changes
- Compare outputs across runs for regression detection
