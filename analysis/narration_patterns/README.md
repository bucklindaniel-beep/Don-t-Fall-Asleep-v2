# Narration Patterns

## Purpose

This folder stores source-safe narration mechanics extracted from approved reference material.

It is used to improve delivery quality, pacing, conversational flow, pause placement, and ElevenLabs-ready narration formatting.

---

## Scope

Allowed:

- pacing mechanics
- pause and breath patterns
- sentence rhythm observations
- conversational transition mechanics
- escalation timing mechanics
- listener-orientation techniques
- delivery patterns that can be generalized

Not allowed:

- copied phrasing
- story details
- source-specific structure replication
- biographical creator imitation
- direct imitation of any creator voice

---

## Intended Use

Narration patterns may inform:

- `/frameworks/15_narration_pacing_system.md`
- `/playbooks/05_narration_delivery_playbook.md`
- `/prompts/04_narration_voice_prompt.md`
- ElevenLabs formatting decisions

Narration patterns must not override story structure, transcript analysis safety rules, or originality rules.

---

## Source Handling Rule

Creators such as MrBallen may be used as adjacent reference material for narration mechanics only.

Use them to study delivery quality, pacing, and engagement rhythm.

Do not use them as direct story-structure templates.
