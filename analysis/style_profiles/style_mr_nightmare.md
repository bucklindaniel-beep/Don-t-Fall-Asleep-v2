# Style Profile — Mr. Nightmare

## Metadata
- Type: Style Profile
- Domain: Voice / Tone / Delivery
- Source Type: YouTube Narration
- Status: active

---

## Core Voice

- calm
- controlled
- conversational
- slightly detached

Narration feels like someone recounting a real experience, not performing.

---

## Sentence Structure

- mix of short and medium sentences
- occasional longer sentences for context
- natural phrasing, not overly segmented

---

## Tone Characteristics

- minimal emotion
- understated delivery
- tension comes from events, not language

---

## Pacing Style

- slow buildup
- tension introduced gradually
- escalation happens in controlled steps

---

## Language Simplicity

- simple vocabulary
- no complex wording
- no poetic phrasing

---

## Narrative Perspective

- first person
- direct recounting
- past tense

---

## Information Control

- delayed explanations
- ambiguity maintained
- details revealed gradually

---

## Engagement Techniques

- curiosity gaps
- repetition with variation
- delayed confirmation of threat

---

## What to Avoid

- dramatic language
- exaggerated reactions
- over-description
- complex sentences

---

## Compression Summary

Calm, grounded narration that builds tension through restraint and gradual escalation.
