# Wordbank — Spatial Awareness

## Purpose
Controls distance, placement, and positional tension.

---

## Entries

### Far (Presence)
- at the edge of the hallway [distance: far, stage: early]
- just beyond the doorway [distance: far, stage: early]
- farther than it should be [distance: far, stage: early]

### Mid (Pattern / Escalation)
- slightly off-center [distance: mid, stage: mid]
- not where it was before [distance: mid, stage: mid]
- closer than before [distance: mid, stage: mid]

### Near (Proximity / Intrusion)
- directly behind him [distance: near, stage: late]
- within arm’s reach [distance: near, stage: late]
- between him and the exit [distance: near, stage: late]

---

## Pairing Rules
- combine with temporal phrases for escalation
- align with entity behavior (observer = far, intrusive = near)

---

## Anti-Repetition
- avoid repeating the same spatial anchor within a scene
