I started noticing it three nights ago.

At first, it was small. I left the kitchen cabinet slightly open before going to bed, and when I woke up, it was closed.

I assumed I forgot.

The next night, I left it open again and documented it with a photo.

In the morning, it was closed again.

The photo still showed it open.

Reality did not match the evidence.

At that point, I stopped interacting with the environment before bed.

I began observing instead.

On the third night, I stayed awake.

Around 2:30 AM, I heard a subtle noise from the kitchen.

It was quiet and deliberate, as if something was trying not to be heard.

I remained still and listened.

I heard the cabinet door slowly close.

I had not opened it that night.
