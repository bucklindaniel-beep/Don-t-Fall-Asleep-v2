## Story 01 — Hotel Notes Under Door Incident

I used to travel a lot for work and most of the time I stayed in cheap but decent hotels because I didn't care about anything except having a bed and a shower. Nothing scary ever happened to me in any hotel before. So I never thought about danger or weird people. That changed during a trip I took for a training event in a small city in Pennsylvania.

I checked into a regular-looking hotel that had a small lobby with old depressing red carpet and a smell that reminded me of my grandma's house. The woman at the front desk gave me a room key and I went up to my room on the third floor. The hallway was very quiet and felt a little empty in a strange way.

My room was pretty normal with a small bed, a desk, and a bathroom. I threw my bag on the bed, turned on the TV and tried to relax. I was tired from driving for hours. So, after showering and watching TV for a bit, I tried to fall asleep.

I woke up in the middle of the night because I heard something sliding on the floor. At first, I thought it was the ice machine or some noise from the hallway. I was half asleep and did not want to get up. But then I heard the soft sliding sound again. And this time it was right by the door.

I turned on the lamp and looked at the door. Something small and white was sticking out from underneath. I got up slowly and picked it up. It was a folded piece of paper. There was handwriting inside, very messy and uneven. It was only a few words telling me that someone was watching me from somewhere inside the hotel.

My heart started beating harder because I had no idea who could have written that. I didn't hear any footsteps after the note appeared and the hallway had been dead silent.
