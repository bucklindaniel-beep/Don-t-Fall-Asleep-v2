### Story 01 — The Falcon Lake Incident (UFO / Paranormal / Credible Encounter)

**Type:** Paranormal / UFO / historical
**Format:** Single long-form narrative
**Setting:** Falcon Lake, Manitoba, Canada; May 20, 1967

**Core Arc:**
Stefan Michalak, a 50-year-old amateur prospector and Polish-born concentration camp survivor, was prospecting near Falcon Lake when he witnessed two disc-shaped craft descend over the lake. One flew off; the other landed 150 feet from him. He approached, heard voices inside, sketched the craft, reached out and touched it — his welding glove melted — and was hit by an exhaust blast from a rotating vent that set him on fire and burned his chest and stomach in a distinctive checkerboard pattern. He staggered back to a hospital. Within 24 hours the story was worldwide news and attracted U.S. and Canadian government investigators. Physical evidence was found at the site. Neither government was able to debunk the incident.
