  3. Narrator runs deep into the woods alone and hides in a tree with a large bush

### Story 03
- **Setting:** Isolated cabin in Canadian forest; no road access; neighboring cabin families within a few miles
- **Narrator Profile:** Adult; lived in cabin with wife and two children for nearly 10 years; small farm and livestock; non-believer in the paranormal
- **Core Event Sequence:**
  1. Late night: cattle flee frantically; narrator spots a very large, motionless deer partially behind a tree, staring at the house
  2. Weeks later: sound of something dragging a stick in a full circle around the cabin; stops
  3. Same deer spotted motionless outside the bedroom window at night
  4. Walking to neighbor's cabin, narrator finds the deer standing on the path 15–20 ft away
  5. Deer's eyes show white around the iris and small pupils — described as distinctly human
  6. Narrator runs to neighbor's cabin in tears
  7. Neighbor, unsurprised, explains Navajo skinwalker beliefs; appears at peace while discussing it
- **Resolution:** No further incidents. Family eventually moves closer to town. Narrator has never disclosed the full encounter to wife or children. Account shared now after nearly 15 years of silence.
- **Key Detail:** Human-like eyes on the deer; cattle fled from it; neighbor's calm deepened narrator's fear
- **Narrator Framing:** Written account; long delay before disclosure; internal compulsion to keep family uninformed
