### Story 02 — Ronald McNair and the Segregated Library (Historical / Inspirational)

**Type:** Historical / human interest
**Format:** Third-person narrative
**Setting:** South Carolina; 1959

**Core Arc:**
Nine-year-old Ronald McNair — a gifted Black boy who wanted to become an astronaut — walked into a segregated (whites-only) library, picked out books on NASA, and refused to leave when the librarian threatened to call police. Police arrived and told the librarian to let him check out the books. Ronald went on to earn a PhD in physics from MIT, became the second African American astronaut, went to space in 1984, and died when the Challenger exploded in 1986. The library that turned him away was later renamed the Ronald McNair Life History Center.
