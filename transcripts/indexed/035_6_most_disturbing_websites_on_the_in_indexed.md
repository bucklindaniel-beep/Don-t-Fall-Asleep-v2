### Story 04 — This Man (thismanproject.com / everDreamThisMan)
- type: internet phenomenon / documented hoax
- subject: "This Man" — recurring dream figure
- url: thismanproject.com
- origin_date: 2008 (website); 2006 (claimed first sighting)
- core_event: Website claims a New York psychiatrist's patient in 2006 sketched a man who appeared repeatedly in her dreams; second patient independently recognized the sketch as someone appearing in their own dreams; psychiatrist shared sketch with colleagues; within months four more patients reported seeing the same man; sketch spread publicly; at least 2,000 people from multiple cities claimed to recognize the man from their dreams; website created 2008; media coverage began 2009; posters with "Ever Dream This Man?" appeared in real-life locations worldwide; website includes multiple portraits and a collection of submitted dream accounts
- key_details:
  - Entire story confirmed as a hoax; described as a marketing campaign; product or client behind campaign never revealed
  - Real individuals came forward claiming to have seen the man in their dreams even after hoax was debunked
  - Website "dream imitation theory": mass media exposure caused people to dream about the face
  - Website "daytime recognition theory": face is so average that dreaming minds substitute it for unremembered faces
- outcome: Hoax confirmed; website remained active; some genuine reported sightings post-debunking
