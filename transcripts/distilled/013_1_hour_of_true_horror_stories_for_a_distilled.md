## Compilation Overview

Whispered Diaries compilation format. Eleven first-person horror stories read aloud, no music between stories, quiet delivery style. All stories are anonymous first-person accounts submitted by audience or sourced from community. Themes cluster around intrusion (domestic, rental, outdoor), luring, predatory setup, and wilderness disappearance. No supernatural content — all grounded, realistic threat scenarios.
