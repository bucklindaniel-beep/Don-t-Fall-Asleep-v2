## Story 01 — Trapped in the Fire

### Content Observations

**Documentation type:** Real-time footage of a fatal event, broadcast publicly by the victim.

**Structural shape:** Short, compressed, no ambiguity. The sequence moves from crisis to failed rescue to collapse in a single continuous arc. There is no investigation — the cause is known, the outcome is confirmed.

**Key constraint:** The men had nowhere to go. One exit, on the wrong side of the building. No alarms. The temporary stairway removed days earlier. The crane facing the wrong direction. Each failure is structural and documented, not a matter of speculation.

**Live stream observation:** The footage exists and is publicly documented. Damonte chose to broadcast. The source notes that his mother and family were among the real-time viewers. The brother arrived on scene during the event itself.

**Structural failures listed in investigation:**

**Emotional dimension:** The source frames the live stream as primarily disturbing because of who was watching — not strangers, but family. The mother viewing her son's final moments in real time. This is presented as the most unsettling element, not the footage itself.

**Sentence:** Life in prison; parole eligible 2040. Conviction came 12,634 days after the disappearance. The source presents this figure explicitly, without commentary.
